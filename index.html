Seaweed - treasure of the ocean

A promising ocean-based solution for mitigating climate change by absorbing carbon and providing lower environmental footprint than animal or land-based alternative protein sources.

Offering a myriad of health benefits, making it a valuable addition to a balanced diet. Rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, seaweed supports overall well-being.

Who we are

Our family has been immersed in the seaweed industry since the 1980s. We manage an extensive network dedicated to sourcing top-quality seaweed, along with operating logistics and storage facilities in Asia.

At Blue Ocean Industries, we play a pivotal role in providing access to premium seaweed products for both consumers and businesses in Europe.
